Who We Are

Engineering Vertical Mobility Since 2009

Safe Reach Engineering is a specialized lift and elevator company based in Dhaka, Bangladesh. We serve residential, commercial, and industrial clients across South Asia and internationally, providing end-to-end solutions from design through delivery.

Certified & Compliant

All installations adhere to international safety standards including EN 81, ASME A17.1, and local BNBC codes.

Global Supply Chain

We import premium elevator components from Europe, China, and South Korea, offering competitive pricing with assured quality.

After-Sales Support

24/7 maintenance and AMC (Annual Maintenance Contract) packages keep your systems running year-round.

What We Do

Comprehensive Lift Solutions

From single-unit installations to large-scale commercial projects and international trade facilitation — we handle the full spectrum.

Passenger Elevators

High-speed, smooth-ride passenger lifts for residential towers, hospitals, hotels, and commercial high-rises. Customizable cabin finishes and control systems.

Cargo & Goods Lifts

Heavy-duty freight elevators rated for loads from 500 kg to 10,000 kg. Ideal for warehouses, factories, and multi-storey retail spaces.

Hydraulic Lifts

Oil-hydraulic systems perfect for low-rise buildings where machine-room-less design and energy efficiency are priorities.

Maintenance & AMC

Preventive maintenance schedules, 24/7 emergency breakdown response, and comprehensive Annual Maintenance Contracts for full peace of mind.

Import & Export

We source and export elevator components globally. Our trade desk handles customs clearance, logistics, and compliance documentation seamlessly.

Consultancy & Design

Our engineers provide site assessments, shaft design, load calculations, and regulatory compliance consulting before any project begins.
